Who We Are

eCorner Canada Ltd. is a Canadian Limited Liability Incorporation established in Alberta, Canada. Our main business currently focuses on GIS related consulting services. The consulting services of eCorner Canada Ltd. include GIS Mapping, GIS Data Management, GIS Online Web Mapping, GIS Analysis, GIS Custom Application, GIS Database Design etc. ECorner Canada Ltd. is a company that has the knowledge and expertise to provide our customers with a full sweet of services designed to build long lasting business relationships.
